RETURNING TO LAX

A public bus leaves every 15 minutes from Santa Monica Place and arrives at LAX. The bus ride is approximately 45 minutes long.

1) From your terminal of arrival, take a Lot C shuttle to the LAX Transit Center. The shuttle stop is at the 2nd sidewalk of the arrivals area.

2) At the LAX Transit Center, you want to take the Santa Monica Rapid 3 bus, also known as the Blue Rapid 3 bus, towards Santa Monica Place. Do not take the Santa Monica Route 3 bus - it takes significantly longer to get to the venue.

3) Get off at the 4th Street and Colorado Avenue stop, which should be the 12th stop after you enter the bus.

4) From there, you will see a huge Bloomingdale's sign - the venue is on the third floor of the mall, located directly above Bloomingdale's.

5) Go to the third floor of the mall (where the Bloomingdale's sign is) and you will arrive at Santa Monica Place L3.

If you get lost, please call Shubhi at 408-429-9472.
